## ðŸš€ How to Use

Using the Gardening Assistant is simple and intuitive.

### 1. Identifying a Plant

1.  Navigate to the **Plant Identifier** tab.
2.  Click the **"Upload a Photo"** button and select an image file of the plant you want to identify.
3.  The AI will analyze the image. This may take a few moments.
4.  Once complete, you will see a detailed card with:
    -   The plant's common name.
    -   A short description and a paragraph about its history.
    -   A list of detailed **Care Steps** covering light, watering, soil, and more.
5.  Your identified plant will be automatically added to your **Identification History** at the bottom of the page.

### 2. Asking Verde for More Tips

1.  After a plant has been identified, a button will appear on the results card: **"Ask Verde for More Tips!"**.
2.  Clicking this button will switch you to the **Gardening Chat** tab.
3.  Verde will greet you with a personalized message about your newly identified plant, ready to answer any follow-up questions you have.

### 3. General Gardening Chat

1.  Navigate to the **Gardening Chat** tab at any time.
2.  Type your plant-related question into the input box at the bottom.
3.  Press Enter or click the send button.
4.  Verde will provide a helpful and enthusiastic response!

### 4. Viewing Your History

1.  On the **Plant Identifier** tab, scroll down below the main upload area.
2.  You will find a grid of all the plants you've previously identified.
3.  Click on any image in the history to instantly bring up its detailed information card again.

Happy gardening!
